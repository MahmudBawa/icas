The History of Our Firm

ICAS is an organisational, human resource and social development consultancy company that has been functioning for over 25 years and was founded by Mohammed Kudu Bawa. The organisation is focused on health, education, agriculture, energy and environment sectors. It is also equipped with personnels that are highly skilled in M&E (including theory-based evaluation, developmental evaluation, participatory monitoring and evaluation), EGRA, LEMA, GALA, as well as impact assessment.
